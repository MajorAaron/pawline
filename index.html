FAQ

Is the demo limited?

Yes — the public demo caps at 15 stops so we don't get crushed by API costs. The full product handles 500+ yards across as many days as you run.

Do I need to share my full customer list?

For the demo, no. For the paid product, you'll upload a CSV. Customer data stays in your Pawline workspace and we never sell or share it.

Does it integrate with QuickBooks / Stripe?

QuickBooks export is on the Pack Pro tier. Stripe sync is rolling out on Kennel Master. If you need something specific, drop your email below and tell us.

How does it know how to route a city it's never seen?

Pawline uses a large language model trained on geographic data plus our own pet-waste-tuned heuristics (gate codes, dog count tiers, weekly vs biweekly cadence). It groups by neighborhood and zip and sequences for drive time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plan, no contracts, prorate on cancellation. We're a small team and we'd rather earn your renewal than trap you.
